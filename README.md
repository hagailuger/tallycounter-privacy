# Privacy Policy for Tally Counter

**Last updated: April 16, 2026**

Hagai Luger ("we", "us", "our") operates the Tally Counter mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

## Information Collection and Use

Tally Counter is designed to be a simple counting tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information that you enter into the App (such as counter names, counter values, or settings).

All counter data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. The information collected by Google AdMob may include:

- Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
- IP address
- App usage data
- General location data (country/region level)

For more information on how Google uses this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by resetting your Advertising ID in your device settings.

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

- **Notifications**: Only used if you enable "Daily Reminder" in Settings, to remind you to update your counters. No notifications are sent without your explicit opt-in.
- **Internet**: Required to display advertisements through Google AdMob.

## Data Security

Since all your counter data is stored locally on your device, the security of this data depends on your device's security. We recommend keeping your device updated and secured with a screen lock.

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
